A very intricate plot that had me questioning every inch throughout the whole story. I honestly did not expect it to end the way it did and I'm very pleased with the direction it went. The beginning wasn't totally up my alley, but it was entertaining enough to stick with, and about halfway through it became exactly my kind of fiction.

I can't help but wonder where the story could go from here. It's not like it ended on any cliff hangers, there was lots of conclusiveness and finality to the ending, lots of lingering questions were answered, but there's still this intriguing "what if?" that I appreciate in a well-designed setting.

An impressive introduction to a new author. I'm very curious about their other books, if they explore this same universe and what that could look like. Or even if they're something entirely different, based on the creativity present in this book I'm sure they would have something new and exciting to share.
