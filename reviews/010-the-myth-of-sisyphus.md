This was some of my first "lengthy" exposure to Camus and I'm incredibly impressed by his writing. I'm excited to discover his other works, and see how they relate with this collection. A lot of my experience with his philosophical inquiry was making connections to other schools of thought, and the similar conclusions being made. This is definitely going to be another one I hope to re-read.
