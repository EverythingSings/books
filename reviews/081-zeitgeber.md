This wasn't fantastic. It was a quick read, so not inconvenient. It wasn't terrible or bad, but it didn't meet my expectations. I didn't know what I was getting into when I bought it, so it was a fresh experience, and it did keep me hooked to the end. Even though it contains some science elements I would consider it more of a mystery book than science fiction. And for people into quirky and creative mysteries then it may just be what they're looking for. But personally it felt a bit lacking, the story was resolved but I still felt like something was missing.
