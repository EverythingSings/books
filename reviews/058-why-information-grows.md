I loved this book. It's a topic that I've been pondering for over four years now, and it was really exciting to find something so relatable. This book is pretty extensive, it has plenty of examples and data to back up the presented arguments. While in a sense it only scratched the surface, it's also one of the most thorough works I've found on the subject. I highly recommend it if you're interested in the phenomena of order, information, or economics.
